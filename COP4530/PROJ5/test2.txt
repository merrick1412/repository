Welcome to the Department of Computer Science at the Florida State University. Our
mission is

    To strive for excellence in creating, applying, and imparting knowledge in
computer science through comprehensive educational programs, research in collaboration
with industry and government, and dissemination through scholarly publications;

    To produce outstanding computer science professionals who have the fundamental
knowledge, problem solving skills, and life long learning capability to advance their
careers.

    To disseminate computer science knowledge to students and professionals in other
disciplines, and

    To provide service to the university community, professional societies, local
communities, the state, and the nation.
